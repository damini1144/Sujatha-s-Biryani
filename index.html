About Sujatha’s Biryani & Curries

At Sujatha’s Biryani & Curries, we bring you the true essence of Andhra and Telangana cuisine. Located in the heart of Manikonda, Hyderabad, our kitchen serves up traditional recipes that have been passed down through generations—with a modern touch of love and care.

From spicy Godavari meals and rich curries to perfectly layered biryanis, every dish is crafted using premium ingredients, fresh ground spices, and authentic cooking techniques.

Whether you’re dining in with family or grabbing a quick takeaway, we promise quality, hygiene, and unbeatable taste—every single time.

Come taste the difference!
