Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07 July 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Order details — Name, mobile number, and selected menu items when you place an order.
  • Basic cookies — Session cookies that remember your visit preferences (see Cookie Policy).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm, prepare, and deliver your food order.
  • To respond to enquiries or feedback you send us.
  • For internal record‑keeping and to improve our services.

3. Data Retention

Order records are kept for up to 12 months for accounting purposes, after which they are securely deleted.

4. Security

We limit access to personal information to authorised staff only. Data is stored on password‑protected devices.

5. Sharing of Information

We never sell or rent your data. We only share details with delivery partners when necessary to fulfil your order.

6. Your Rights

You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing orders@sujathabiryani.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
